👋 About Me

I am currently pursuing my Bachelor of Applied Technology in Cybersecurity, driven by a strong passion for protecting systems, data, and organizational integrity. Through my education and hands-on training, I am developing technical competencies in network security, threat detection and incident response, vulnerability assessment, security frameworks such as NIST, and foundational scripting and system analysis. I am particularly interested in how secure architectures are designed to proactively mitigate risk and create resilient, scalable environments.

My professional background in operational leadership and IT logistics has provided a strong foundation for a career in cybersecurity. In high-volume retail and production environments, I was responsible for risk mitigation, regulatory compliance, asset protection, and audit readiness. I routinely identified process vulnerabilities, implemented structured controls, and ensured adherence to corporate and regulatory standards. While working in IT logistics, I gained exposure to asset lifecycle management and infrastructure accountability, reinforcing the importance of visibility, documentation, and control — all critical elements of a strong security posture.

I bring a disciplined, risk-aware mindset combined with leadership experience and a growing technical skill set. I thrive in environments that require structured thinking, cross-functional collaboration, and decisive action under pressure. As I continue advancing toward a career in cybersecurity — with a long-term goal of contributing to secure system design and architecture — I am committed to continuous learning, operational excellence, and building security solutions that are resilient by design.
